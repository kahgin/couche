# About Couché
Couché, a Malaysian candle brand established in 2021, is recognized for its high-quality candles with unique fragrances. The brand is noteworthy for obtaining certification from the International Fragrance Association (IFRA), demonstrating its dedication to maintaining top-notch product quality and safety standards.
