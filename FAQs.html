General

Our candles are made from natural soy wax, phthalate-free fragrance oils & a cotton based wick.

  • Vegan friendly
  • Cleaner, safer & longer burn
  • Non-toxic
  • Excellent fragrance carrier

Our candles are infused with premium fine fragrance oils that are safe to use, phthalate-free and IFRA (International Fragrance Association) approved.

Candle care

First, make sure the pool of wax extends to the outside edges of the tin. We recommend burning it for at least 3-4 hours.

Do trim your wick to at least 5mm before every use to prevent mushrooming wick.

Avoid blowing the candle's flame directly with your mouth as it will cause wax splashing and wicks drifting off-center. Do use a sniffer or the tin can lid to extinguish it instead.

Store it at a cool dry place away from children, pets, and flammable items. Do make sure to place the candle on a heat-resistant base to avoid any heat marks.
